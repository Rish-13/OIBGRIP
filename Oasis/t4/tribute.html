Bhagat Singh Sandhu

देखना है ज़ोर कितना बाजु-ए-कातिल में है।।

  • Born - September 28, 1907 Lyallpur, western Punjab, India
  • Died - March 23, 1931, Lahore [now in Pakistan].
  • Known for - Bhagat Singh remains a significant figure in Indian iconography to the present day. His memory, however, defies categorisation and presents problems for various groups that might try to appropriate it.
  • His works - Bhagat Singh wrote Why I Am an Atheist, Jail Diary and Other Writings, and To Young Political Workers, among other books.
